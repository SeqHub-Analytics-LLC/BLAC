Why you should hire BLAC

Talent Sourcing

There is a war for talent, especially STEM and diversity candidates STEM occupations are forecast to grow at a rate over 76% higher than non-STEM job growth nationwide. Currently the demographics are 70 percent white and 65 percent male.

Profitability

Missed opportunities to create a diverse work space cost the U.S an estimated $1.05 trillion per year. Diverse leadership increases profitability. Research shows that a technical lead resource replacement can cost a company up to 250% of an annual salary.

Culture

Building an inclusive culture attracts, empowers and retains diverse talent. 82% of companies say D&I helps them to attract and retain talent, and 62% say it drives employee engagement.
